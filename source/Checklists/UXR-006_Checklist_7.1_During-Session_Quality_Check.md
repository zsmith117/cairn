# During-Session Quality Check

**Source:** UXR-006 SOP
**Section:** 7.1
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** January 2026

---

Monitor quality in real-time:

| Check | Standard | How to Monitor |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Capturing key quotes** | At least 3-5 notable quotes per session | Mark quotes as you capture them; use "QUOTE" tag |
| **Noting behaviors** | Behavioral observations beyond just words | Include tone, reactions, body language notes |
| **Timestamping critical moments** | Key moments have timestamps | Note time for important moments; mark for later review |
| **Maintaining pace** | Keeping up with conversation | If falling behind, switch to shorter paraphrase; focus on key info |
| **Coverage** | All major topics addressed | Check off guide sections as you cover them |

**Mid-Session Self-Check:**

At transition points, quickly assess:
- Am I capturing enough detail?
- Am I getting key quotes?
- Am I noting behaviors and context?
- Am I falling behind? Need to adjust approach?
