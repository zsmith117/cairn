# During-Communication Quality Check

**Source:** UXR-008 SOP
**Section:** 7.2
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** January 2026

---

Monitor during presentation/discussion:

| Check | What to Observe |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Understanding** | Are stakeholders following? Confused faces? Good questions? |
| **Engagement** | Are they engaged or distracted? Taking notes? |
| **Acceptance** | Are they accepting findings or resisting? |
| **Pace** | Are you moving too fast or slow? Running over time? |
| **Interaction** | Are they asking questions? Discussing? Silent? |
| **Misunderstanding** | Are they misinterpreting findings? Need clarification? |

**Mid-Presentation Adjustments:**

- If confusion: Slow down; add clarification; ask if they're following
- If disengagement: Speed up; add interaction; tell story
- If resistance: Acknowledge concerns; show evidence; invite discussion
- If running long: Skip nice-to-have slides; focus on priorities; shorten Q&A
