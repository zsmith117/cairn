# Impact Quality Check

**Source:** UXR-008 SOP
**Section:** 7.4
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** January 2026

---

3-6 months after research:

```
IMPACT ASSESSMENT CHECKLIST

AWARENESS ACHIEVED:
[ ] Target stakeholders saw/heard research
[ ] Broader team aware of key findings
[ ] Research is referenced and remembered

UNDERSTANDING ACHIEVED:
[ ] Stakeholders accurately understand findings
[ ] No major misinterpretations
[ ] Context and limitations understood

CONSIDERATION IN DECISIONS:
[ ] Research discussed in relevant meetings
[ ] Findings inform decision-making conversations
[ ] Research is cited as factor in decisions

ACTIONS TAKEN:
[ ] Recommendations acted upon (fully or partially)
[ ] Research influenced product roadmap
[ ] Design/product decisions reflect insights

OUTCOMES MEASURED:
[ ] User experience metrics tracked
[ ] Business metrics tracked
[ ] Research value demonstrated

RELATIONSHIP BUILT:
[ ] Stakeholder trust in research increased
[ ] Research seen as valuable by team
[ ] Future research opportunities identified

RESEARCH ROI:
[ ] Research impact documented
[ ] Value demonstrated
[ ] Lessons learned captured

IMPACT LEVEL:
[ ] High - Clear decision/action influence + measurable outcomes
[ ] Medium - Influenced consideration; some actions taken
[ ] Low - Awareness achieved but limited action
[ ] None - No observable impact

If low/no impact: [Root cause analysis & improvement plan]
```
