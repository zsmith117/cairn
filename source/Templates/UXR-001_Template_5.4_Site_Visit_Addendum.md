**Site Visit Terms and Conditions Addendum**

This addendum applies to research sessions conducted at [Participant's Organization] facilities.

**Safety and Conduct**

I ([Researcher Name], representing [Company Name]) agree to:

- Comply with all site safety rules, protocols, and signage
- Complete any required safety orientation or training
- Wear all required personal protective equipment (PPE)
- Remain with my designated escort at all times if required
- Not enter restricted areas without explicit authorization
- Report any safety incidents or concerns immediately

**Recording and Photography**

I understand and agree that:

- Photography, video, and audio recording are [permitted only in designated areas / prohibited / permitted with prior approval]
- Any recordings or photographs taken are subject to review by [Organization] before removal from premises
- [Organization] may request deletion of any recordings that capture proprietary processes, trade secrets, or restricted areas
- I will not publish or share images of [Organization's] facilities, equipment, or personnel without prior written consent

**Proprietary Information**

I acknowledge that I may observe proprietary processes, equipment, or information during this site visit. I agree to:

- Treat all observations as confidential unless explicitly told otherwise
- Not disclose specific details of proprietary processes in external reports
- Describe observations in generalized terms that do not reveal competitive intelligence
- Consult with [Organization contact] before publishing any findings that reference specific observations from this visit

**Assumption of Risk**

I understand that [industrial/manufacturing/laboratory] environments involve inherent risks. I agree to follow all safety instructions and acknowledge that [Company Name] and [Organization] are not liable for injuries resulting from my failure to follow safety protocols.
