# Stakeholder Workshop Agenda

**Source:** UXR-008 SOP
**Section:** 5.4
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** January 2026

---

```
RESEARCH WORKSHOP: [STUDY NAME]

===================================================================

PURPOSE: Collaborative synthesis and decision-making based on
         research findings

DURATION: 2-3 hours

PARTICIPANTS:
- Research team: [Names]
- Product/Design: [Names]
- Engineering: [Names]
- Stakeholders: [Names]

MATERIALS NEEDED:
- Research findings presentation
- Printed key quotes
- Whiteboard or digital board (Miro/Mural)
- Sticky notes and markers (if in-person)
- Pre-read: Research summary sent 2 days before

===================================================================

AGENDA

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

10:00-10:15 (15 min) | INTRODUCTION & CONTEXT

- Welcome and goals for session
- Brief research overview (5 min)
  - What we studied and why
  - Who we talked to
  - High-level findings preview
- How today will work
- Ground rules for discussion

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

10:15-10:45 (30 min) | KEY FINDINGS PRESENTATION

- Researcher presents main findings
- Evidence and examples
- Initial reactions (save deep discussion)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

10:45-11:00 (15 min) | BREAK

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

11:00-11:30 (30 min) | INSIGHTS EXPLORATION

Activity: Small Group Discussion

Divide into 2-3 groups. Each group discusses:
- What surprised you?
- What confirms what we suspected?
- What questions does this raise?
- What are the implications for [product/strategy]?

Each group reports back (5 min each)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

11:30-12:15 (45 min) | COLLABORATIVE IDEATION

Activity: "How Might We...?" Brainstorm

Based on findings, generate ideas:

1. SILENT IDEA GENERATION (10 min)
   - Each person writes ideas on sticky notes
   - Format: "How might we [address need/problem]?"
   - Aim for 10+ ideas per person

2. SHARE & GROUP (15 min)
   - Post ideas on board
   - Silent grouping by theme
   - Name the themes

3. DISCUSS & PRIORITIZE (20 min)
   - Discuss interesting clusters
   - Dot vote on priorities
   - Identify top 3-5 opportunities

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

12:15-12:30 (15 min) | BREAK

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

12:30-13:00 (30 min) | DECISION-MAKING

Activity: Roadmap Implications

Discuss and decide:

1. IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (5 min)
   - What can we do now based on research?
   - Who owns what?

2. ROADMAP IMPACT (15 min)
   - Does this change our priorities?
   - What should we add/remove/modify?
   - What's the sequencing?

3. OPEN QUESTIONS (10 min)
   - What do we still need to learn?
   - What additional research is needed?
   - What assumptions need testing?

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

13:00-13:15 (15 min) | NEXT STEPS & CLOSING

- Recap decisions made
- Action items with owners and dates
- Follow-up plans
- Thank participants

===================================================================

FACILITATOR NOTES

PRE-WORKSHOP PREPARATION:
[ ] Send research summary 2 days before
[ ] Book room/set up video call
[ ] Prepare materials (presentation, supplies)
[ ] Set up digital board if remote
[ ] Print key quotes and findings

FACILITATION TIPS:
- Keep to time - use timers
- Encourage all voices - call on quiet participants
- Capture everything - assign scribe
- Stay neutral - don't lead discussion
- Focus on user needs, not solutions (initially)
- Create safe space for dissenting opinions

POST-WORKSHOP:
[ ] Document decisions and action items
[ ] Send summary within 24 hours
[ ] Follow up on action items
[ ] Track impact of decisions made

===================================================================
```
