# Phase 5: Delivery & Close

Sharing findings and ensuring knowledge is preserved for the organization.

---

## Phase Overview

**Objective:** Effectively communicate research findings to stakeholders and archive insights for future reference.

**Duration:** Typically 1–2 weeks for deliverable creation and presentation.

**Key Outcomes:**

- Research deliverables created (report, presentation, etc.)
- Findings presented to stakeholders
- Research archived in repository
- Project formally closed

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Deliverable Creation

Create appropriate deliverables:

- Determine format(s) needed (report, deck, video, etc.)
- Structure around key insights
- Include methodology summary
- Add supporting evidence
- Design for your audience

> Reference [UXR-008: Stakeholder Communication](#sop/UXR-008/procedure) for deliverable formats and best practices.

### 2. Internal Review

Before stakeholder delivery:

- Review deliverables with team
- Check for accuracy and clarity
- Validate recommendations are actionable
- Prepare for potential questions

### 3. Stakeholder Presentation

Share findings effectively:

- Schedule appropriate meeting format
- Present insights and recommendations
- Facilitate discussion
- Capture feedback and questions
- Document decisions and next steps

### 4. Follow-up

After initial presentation:

- Distribute deliverables
- Address follow-up questions
- Track recommendation adoption
- Support implementation if needed

### 5. Repository Archive

Preserve knowledge:

- Upload to research repository
- Apply appropriate taxonomy/tags
- Link to related research
- Add searchable metadata

> Reference [UXR-009: Research Repository](#sop/UXR-009/procedure) for archiving standards and taxonomy.

### 6. Project Close

Formally close the project:

- Update Research Project Tracker status
- Conduct retrospective if applicable
- Document lessons learned
- Celebrate completion

## Phase Completion Checklist

Before marking project complete:

- [ ] Deliverables created and reviewed
- [ ] Findings presented to stakeholders
- [ ] Deliverables distributed
- [ ] Research archived in repository
- [ ] Follow-up questions addressed
- [ ] Project retrospective completed (if applicable)
- [ ] Research Project Tracker updated to `Completed`

---

## Project Complete!

Once Phase 5 is complete:

1. Update your project's `Current Phase` to `Completed`
2. Add `P5-Complete` to `Phase Progress`
3. Archive project documentation
